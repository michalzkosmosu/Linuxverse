I've collected various feedback on Pinebook Pro in one place

Pinebook Pro is a $200 ARM-based laptop from Pine64. It was created as an alternative to mid-ranged Chromebooks. It runs Linux. It has a 14" IPS LCD, magnesium alloy shell and 64/128GB of eMMC storage.
