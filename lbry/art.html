List of art and entertainment channels on LBRY

I love LBRY, a decentralized replacement for YouTube. I use it on a daily basis and I'm happy that more and more creators are appearing there. I already covered the Linux channels, now it's time for art and entertainment.

@DUST – If you like the Orwellian spirit, there's something for you. 'Dust' presents thought-provoking science fiction content, exploring the future of humanity through the lens of science and technology.

@samuel_earp_artist – This is Samuel Earp's channel, New Zealand landscape artist and online painting teacher.

@TheTimeLapseLord – This man creates incredible timelapses with crazy colors added in post-production.

If you know another entertainment and art channels on LBRY, please send me an e-mail. I'll update the list.
